Learn With Experts

Overview:
Join us for an exciting and insightful event, "Learn with Experts," where industry professionals and subject matter specialists come together to share their knowledge and experiences. This interactive session is designed to provide practical insights, expert guidance, and real-world applications in various fields.

Whether you're a student, a working professional, or an enthusiast eager to expand your knowledge, this event offers the perfect opportunity to learn from the best. Engage in discussions, ask questions, and gain valuable takeaways that can help you grow personally and professionally. Don't miss this chance to connect, learn, and be inspired!
Rules and Regulations:
To ensure a smooth and productive experience for all participants, please follow these rules and regulations:

Registration Required – All attendees must register before the event to secure their participation.Participant should join the event on time to avoid disruptions.

Respect and Decorum – Maintain a respectful and professional attitude towards speakers, organizers, and fellow attendees.

No Disruptive Behavior – Avoid unnecessary interruptions, side conversations, or any form of misconduct during the session.

Mobile Phones on Silent – Keep mobile devices on silent mode to minimize distractions.

Q&A Etiquette – Raise your hand or use the designated platform to ask questions during the interactive sessions.

No Recording Without Permission – Audio or video recording of the event is not allowed unless permitted by the organizers.

Follow Event Guidelines – Adhere to any additional instructions provided by the organizers to ensure a seamless event experience.

Feedback Submission – Participants are encouraged to provide feedback to help improve future events.

Zero Tolerance for Harassment – Any form of harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated and may lead to immediate removal from the event.!
Winning and Scoring criteria:
If the event includes competitions, quizzes, or interactive challenges, the following scoring criteria will be applied to determine winners:

Scoring Criteria

  • Accuracy (40%) – Correct answers or solutions provided during quizzes, Q&A rounds, or challenges.
  • Participation (20%) – Active engagement in discussions, asking insightful questions, and contributing valuable inputs.
  • Creativity & Problem-Solving (20%) – Innovative thinking and unique approaches in case studies, problem-solving tasks, or presentations.
  • Presentation & Communication (10%) – Clarity, confidence, and effectiveness in presenting ideas.
  • Time Management (10%) – Completing tasks, quizzes, or activities within the given timeframe.

Winning Criteria:

  • The participant with the highest cumulative score across all evaluation parameters will be declared the winner.
  • In case of a tie, an additional tie-breaker round or expert evaluation may be conducted.
  • Special recognition may be given for categories like "Best Question Asked," "Most Active Participant," or "Most Innovative Solution."
